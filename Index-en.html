Professional Summary

PhD in Psychology and researcher with over 15 years of experience leading projects in sexual health, with a strong focus on innovation, technology, and wellbeing. I earned my PhD from the University of Granada and have directed multiple clinical studies, contributed to public policy, and advised top brands like Durex, UNIQ, and Sen Íntimo in the design, validation, and improvement of intimate products.

I have authored more than 60 scientific articles published in top-tier journals such as Journal of Sexual Medicine, Archives of Sexual Behavior, Journal of Sex Research, and Journal of Sexual and Marital Therapy. I founded and directed the first human sexuality lab in Latin America (SexLab KL) and served as editor-in-chief of a scientific journal for nine years. My work bridges academia and real-world impact, supporting consumers, companies, and healthcare professionals alike.

My approach blends scientific evidence, design thinking, and product vision, aiming for tangible results, social impact, and competitive brand positioning.
